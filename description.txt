[hr]

[b]About This Mod[/b]  
Darkly's Enemy Expansion is an enemy expansion mod focused on adding more enemies, stronger variants, and special enemy types to Terraria.  
Its goal is to expand combat encounters across different biomes, progression stages, and events, making exploration feel more varied and challenging.
